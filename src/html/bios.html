Meet the Band

Harry Styles

Harry, known by his close friends as Harvey, started a new band with childhood friends after leaving One Direction. Harry is 27-year-old heart throb who's love for music has swept the nation off their feet.

Niall Horan

Niall was close friends with Harry growing up and after their boyband fame created a lowkey rock band to continue their love of music. Niall is 27 years old.

Shawn Mendes

Shawn is the cement that keeps the group together. He was penpals with his band mates growing up and they took their passion for music and well known fame to make their own impact on the world. Shawn is 22 years old.
